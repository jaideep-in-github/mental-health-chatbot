About This Project

Purpose

This Mental Health Support Bot was created to provide a supportive, non-judgmental space where users can express their feelings and receive compassionate responses. While not a replacement for professional mental health care, this chatbot aims to:

  • Provide immediate emotional support
  • Offer basic coping strategies
  • Direct users to appropriate resources when needed
  • Reduce stigma around discussing mental health

How It Works

This chatbot uses natural language processing technology from Hugging Face's transformers library, specifically the DialoGPT model. The system has been designed to:

  • Recognize common mental health concerns
  • Respond with empathy and understanding
  • Provide appropriate suggestions based on the conversation
  • Flag potential crisis situations and provide emergency resources

Important Disclaimer

This chatbot is not a substitute for professional mental health care. If you're experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact a mental health professional, go to your nearest emergency room, or call national crisis resources like:

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: Call or text 988
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741

Privacy

User conversations are logged anonymously for the purpose of improving the chatbot's responses. No personally identifiable information is stored. Each session is given a unique identifier that is not linked to any personal information.

Note: This is an AI assistant and not a replacement for professional mental health support.

If you're in crisis, please call or text 988 to reach the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline.
